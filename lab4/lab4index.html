here is our first outline on how to make pasta from scratch:

  1. Get ingredients
      2. - Two cups of flour
      - 2 large eggs
      - 3 egg yolks
  2. Form pasta dough
      3. Make well with flour
      Add eggs to flour and mix with fork
  3. knead dough for 8-10 mins
  4. Let the dough rest for at least 30 minutes
  5. Using a pasta machine or rolling pin, roll the dough out lengthwise slightly
  6. On a lightly floured surface, fold the pasta dough in thirds
  7. Repeat these 2 steps until the pasta dough is smooth
  8. Roll the dough out until it is around 1/16 inch thick
  9. Cut pasta into sheets, around 12-14 inches long
  10. Dust both sides of the dough with flour
  11. After cutting to desired shape, stack pasta to the side and cover with a clean, dampened kitchen towel
